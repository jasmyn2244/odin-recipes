Crisply Rice Wraps

Description

These spicy cruchy savory wraps are the perfect meal for a vegitarian craving a meal packed with flavor!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • tofu
  • seaweed sheets
  • siracha
  • soy sauch or tamari

Directions

  1. Cook rice
  2. Mix sauces with tofu
  3. Wrap rice, tofu, and seaweed in rice paper
  4. Fry in pan with oil
  5. Dip in firecracker dipping sauce
  6. Enjoy!
