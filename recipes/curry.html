Curry

Description

This curry is just as tastey as your local Thai restaurant! Its creamy, comforting, and packed with flavor and veggies!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • tofu or chicken
  • bell peppers
  • onion
  • green chili paste
  • fresh ginger
  • fresh garlic
  • limes
  • bamboo shoots
  • coconut milk

Directions

  1. Cook rice
  2. In pan, sate ginger garlic and curry paste
  3. In another pan, saute the chicken and veggies
  4. Add coconut milk, chicken and veggies to curry paste pan
  5. Allow curry to simmer for 5 mins-2hrs
  6. Enjoy!
