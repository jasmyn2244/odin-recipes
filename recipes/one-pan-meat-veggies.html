One Pan Veggies and Sausage

Description

This recipe is super easy but super healthy. You'll have a rainbow of veggies mixed with savory sausage for a satisfying and filling meal with only one dish to wash!

Ingredients

  • Free range organic polish sausage
  • Brussel sproutes
  • Broccli
  • Red onion
  • Bell Pepper
  • Red Beet
  • Fingerling Potatoes

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425
  2. Chop potatoes and beets
  3. Toss them in oil and seasoning, pour onto pan, put in oven for 15 mins
  4. Chop the rest of the veggies and sausage, toss in oild and seasoning, add to pan and cook for another 15-20 mins
  5. Enjoy!
